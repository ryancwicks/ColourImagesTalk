Tech Talk

Colour Images

Presented by Ryan Wicks

Overview

This talk will cover the basics of how digital images are represented and stored.

Topics will include:

  • Grayscale Images
  • Colour Images - Bayer Filters
  • Colour Representation
    • RGB
    • HSV
    • YUV
  • Image Formats
    • Uncompressed (raw, bmp)
    • Compressed (jpg, png)
    • Tagged (tif, geotiff)
